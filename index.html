About P.M.R Nursery

Building a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Established with a vision to provide holistic early childhood education, P.M.R Nursery School offers a safe, engaging, and culturally rich environment.

Our mission is to foster curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child, preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow while letting them enjoy the magic of childhood today.

  • Safe & Secure Campus
  • Play-way Teaching Method
  • Regular Health Checkups
